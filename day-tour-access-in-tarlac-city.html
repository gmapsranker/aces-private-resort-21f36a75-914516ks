Frequently Asked Questions About Day Tour Access

How far in advance should I book a day tour at Aces Private Resort, and what is the reservation process?
We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance, especially if you’re planning to visit on weekends or during peak months (March–June and December). A 50% deposit is required to secure your slot, with the remaining balance due on the day of your visit. Walk-ins are not accepted as we operate exclusively by reservation to ensure privacy for each group.

What happens if it rains on the day of our booking? Do you offer any guarantees or rescheduling options?
We understand that weather in Tarlac can be unpredictable, especially during the rainy season (June–November). If there is heavy rain or a government-issued weather advisory that makes travel unsafe, we offer one free rescheduling within 60 days of your original booking, subject to availability. For light rain, our indoor lounge and covered areas will remain accessible so your group can still enjoy the resort. Please inform us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule due to weather.
